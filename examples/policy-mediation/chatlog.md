### 2026-03-18 09:00:00 - FACILITATOR

Welcome to the policy deliberation on the proposed **Data Retention and Access Transparency Act (DRATA)**.

**Summary of the proposal:**
- Requires all companies processing personal data of EU/UK residents to retain a complete access log for **5 years**
- Access logs must record: who accessed the data, when, from what system, and for what stated purpose
- Individuals have the right to request their full access log within 30 days
- Non-compliance: fines up to 4% of global annual revenue (aligned with GDPR)
- Implementation deadline: 18 months from enactment

**Participants:**
- **ECON** — economic impact and incentives
- **CIVIL-LIB** — rights, privacy, and legal framework
- **INFRA** — technical feasibility and operational requirements

ECON, please open with an economic assessment of the proposal. Others, hold your responses until ECON has posted.
