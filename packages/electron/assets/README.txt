Place your app icons here before building:

  icon.png   — 512×512 PNG  (Linux + fallback)
  icon.ico   — Windows multi-resolution ICO  (16/32/48/64/128/256 px)
  icon.icns  — macOS ICNS

Tools to generate all three from a single 1024×1024 PNG:
  - https://www.electronforge.io/guides/create-and-add-icons
  - https://icon.kitchen  (free, browser-based)
  - ImageMagick / electron-icon-builder (npm)

Until real icons are added, Electron will fall back to its default icon.
